We use cookies, analytics tools, and similar technologies to improve user experience. For details, see our Cookie Notice.

Your data may be transferred outside your country (e.g., US, EU, Singapore). Safeguards like Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) are applied where required.

We keep your personal data as long as you have an account. Logs and analytics are stored up to 12 months, unless legally required longer. Payment records may be retained for tax/audit purposes.

7. Security

We use encryption, secure hosting, and restricted access to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure.

8. Children’s Privacy

Thinkr is designed for children aged 5 years and above. We are committed to protecting children’s privacy and complying with all applicable laws including COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act) in the US and GDPR-K in the EU.

We may collect limited information necessary to provide our services, such as:

  • Non-personal usage data (e.g., app activity, preferences).
  • Basic login credentials if parents create/manage an account.
  • AI inputs submitted by the child during app use.

We do not knowingly collect personal information such as full names, addresses, contact details, or identifiers directly from children without parental consent.

Parental Rights:

  • Parents/guardians can review the information we hold about their child.
  • They can request correction or deletion of their child’s data.
  • They can withdraw consent at any time by contacting us.

If we learn that we have collected personal data from a child without verifiable parental consent, we will delete it immediately.
